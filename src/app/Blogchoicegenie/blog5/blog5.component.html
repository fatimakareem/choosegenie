False Beliefs

Some have misconceptions that, when they choose to switch to an electricity plan through new electricity provider, they might be a period of no electricity.

It doesn’t work like that in states with deregulated electricity.

Utility companies are under an obligation bring electricity unless of course the power is disconnected for whatever reason mainly non-payment.
